AI Ping Pong







Instructions

  1. Make sure your device is straight. Stay 3-4 feet away from the device.
  2. Click the start button. You should see a red dot on your right wrist.
  3. Move your right wrist up and down to move the paddle.
  4. Try to hit the ball past the computer's paddle.
  5. You can always restart and try again. Have fun!
